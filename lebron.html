The Big 3 There's no way to look back on James' career without mentioning the infamous “Decision” television event that led to the formation of the Miami Heats Big Three. James, Dwyane Wade and Chris Bosh joined forces and promised not just one title, but multiple NBA championships. Though they fell short of becoming the modern-day version of Bill Russell's Boston Celtics dynasty, the Heat made four trips to the NBA Finals in four years with the Big Three intact.

LeBron James has a stake in Liverpool FC Liverpool FC is quite a famous club in the English Premier League. Not many know that LeBron James owns stakes in this English Soccer Club. In 2011 LeBron James then playing for Cleveland Cavaliers bought a 2% stake in Liverpool FC for approximately $6.5 million. According to data from Forbes, in 2020, Liverpool is valued at $4.1 billion, that means that James' stake in the club is now worth around $44 million.

Lebron James is Ambidextrous James shoots primarily with his right hand but he is ambidextrous so can also shoot lefty. It's possible that no athlete has stood in a brighter spotlight from such a young age as four-time NBA MVP LeBron James.
