EARVIN "MAGIC" JOHNSON

5X NBA CHAMPION!!

NBA Finals MVP in rookie season Magic won a championship and NBA Finals MVP in his rookie season. He won four more championships with the Lakers in the 1980s. Johnson retired in 1991 very abruptly after announcing that he had contracted HIV. He returned to play in the 1992 All-Star Game and won the All-Star MVP award. He retired again for four years and returned in 1996 to play 32 games for the Lakers before he finally retired for the last time.

Magic Johnson's love for basketball was influenced by his parents Magic Johnson was born on 14th August 1959 and was schooled at Everett high school and Michigan State University. He was influenced by his parents who both loved basketball and grew up among his nine sisters and brothers. The love for basketball began for Magic Johnson since his upbringing to his infamous rivalry between Larry Bird and himself, but above all, Magic Johnson has never disappointed.

Magic Johnson preferred to go by his nickname The name Magic is in quotes, which means that is a stage name that he was given as a legendary American professional who participated in the NBA for more than a decade. Magic Johnson lived up to his stage name, rather his nickname. His career life has revolved around him being a basketball player and an entrepreneur as well.
